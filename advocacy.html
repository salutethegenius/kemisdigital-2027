Advocacy

At the Bahamas Digital Marketing Association (BDMA), advocacy is at the core of our mission. We work tirelessly to advance the interests of digital marketers, support industry growth, and promote fair practices across the digital marketing landscape in the Bahamas. BDMA serves as the voice of digital marketing professionals, engaging with policymakers, businesses, and industry leaders to create a thriving environment for digital innovation.

Our Advocacy Goals

Promoting Industry Standards

BDMA advocates for ethical standards and transparency within digital marketing practices. We work to ensure that marketers adhere to guidelines that promote trust, accountability, and integrity in every campaign and engagement.

Digital Literacy and Education

We are committed to increasing digital literacy across the Bahamas. By supporting digital education initiatives, we aim to empower individuals and businesses to leverage digital tools effectively, benefiting the wider community and economy.

Recent Initiatives

BDMA is engaged with local government and regulatory bodies to ensure that new policies reflect the unique needs of the digital marketing sector. We provide recommendations, share industry insights, and contribute to the development of fair regulations.

Get Involved

BDMA's advocacy work is made possible by the support of our members. Here's how you can participate:

Join a Committee

BDMA offers various committees focused on areas like education, policy, and industry standards. Join a committee to lend your voice and expertise to our advocacy efforts.

Support Our Advocacy Work

As a non-profit organization, BDMA relies on the dedication of our members and the support of our partners to advance our mission. Consider supporting our advocacy work through donations or corporate sponsorships, allowing us to expand our impact and continue representing the interests of digital marketers in the Bahamas.

Together, we can shape the future of digital marketing in the Bahamas. Join us in advocating for a fair, innovative, and ethical digital landscape.
