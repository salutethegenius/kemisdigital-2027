Our Mission

At BDMA, we aim to:

  • Foster a vibrant community of digital marketing professionals.
  • Provide access to training, certifications, and resources.
  • Set and promote ethical standards in digital marketing practices.
  • Advocate for the industry, representing members in matters of policy and best practices.

Leadership

Our association is led by President Kenneth C. Moncur, CIPM, a respected figure in digital marketing with a deep dedication to empowering professionals and setting the standard for excellence in the industry. Under his leadership, our executive team guides BDMA's vision and initiatives, ensuring we remain at the forefront of digital innovation and professional development.

Our Executive Team

BDMA's executive team brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to support our members. Together, we are dedicated to providing unparalleled resources, networking opportunities, and insights into the evolving world of digital marketing. Our team's expertise spans a broad spectrum of digital marketing disciplines, from content strategy and social media to data analytics and e-commerce.
