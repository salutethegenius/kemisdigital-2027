Membership

Welcome to the Bahamas Digital Marketing Association (BDMA) Membership page! As a BDMA member, you join a dynamic community of digital marketing professionals committed to learning, collaboration, and elevating industry standards. We offer multiple membership tiers, each designed to suit the needs of digital marketers at every stage of their career, from students to industry veterans and corporate partners.

Why Join BDMA?

As a BDMA member, you gain access to invaluable resources, industry insights, and a supportive network dedicated to your success. Our members enjoy:

  • Professional Development: Expand your skills through workshops, webinars, and certifications tailored to the latest digital marketing trends.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with like-minded professionals, potential partners, and industry leaders.
  • Access to Industry Insights: Stay ahead with our library of research reports, case studies, and best practice guides.
  • Advocacy: Be represented in conversations that shape the future of digital marketing in the Bahamas.
  • Exclusive Discounts: Enjoy special rates on industry events, BDMA certifications, and partner products and services.

Membership Tiers

BDMA offers tiered memberships to accommodate various levels of experience, commitment, and interest. Explore our options below to find the best fit for your professional goals.

Student Membership

Who It's For: High school, college, or university students studying marketing, communications, or related fields.

Benefits:

  • Access to entry-level workshops and webinars.
  • Eligibility to apply for BDMA scholarships and internship programs.
  • Inclusion in the BDMA student networking group.
  • Opportunities to gain experience by volunteering at BDMA events.

Annual Fee: $25

Individual Professional Membership

Who It's For: Working professionals in digital marketing, from freelancers and consultants to full-time employees looking to grow their careers.

Benefits:

  • Full access to all BDMA events, workshops, and certifications at member rates.
  • Access to BDMA's exclusive job board and career resources.
  • Opportunity to participate in BDMA's mentorship program (as a mentor or mentee).
  • Listing in the BDMA membership directory for increased visibility.

Annual Fee: $100

Corporate Membership

Who It's For: Businesses and agencies looking to support their teams' professional growth, increase their visibility, and connect with potential clients and collaborators.

Benefits:

  • Up to five individual memberships for company representatives.
  • Discounts on BDMA training and workshops for all employees.
  • Logo placement and acknowledgment on the BDMA website.
  • Access to exclusive corporate networking events and roundtable discussions.
  • Opportunities for sponsorship and promotional partnerships with BDMA.

Annual Fee: $500

Lifetime Membership

Who It's For: Established professionals committed to long-term engagement and support of BDMA's mission.

Benefits:

  • Lifetime access to BDMA events, workshops, and resources.
  • VIP access to select events and lifetime recognition on the BDMA website.
  • Opportunity to contribute to BDMA leadership and advisory committees.
  • Exclusive networking events and insider access to BDMA's annual conference.

One-Time Fee: $1,000

How to Join

Becoming a BDMA member is easy! Simply select your membership tier and complete the online application form. Once you submit your application, our team will review it, and you'll receive confirmation within a few days. We can't wait to welcome you to the BDMA community!
