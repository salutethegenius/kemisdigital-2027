Digital Marketing Professional Development Course

Bahamas Baptist University College

Start Date: January 18th, 2024

Duration: 12 weeks

Join BDMA President Kenneth C. Moncur, CIPM at the Bahamas Baptist University College (BBUC) for a comprehensive Digital Marketing Professional Development Course. This 12-week course is tailored for individuals seeking to gain foundational knowledge and practical skills in digital marketing.

Course Topics Include:

  • Digital marketing fundamentals
  • Social media and content strategy
  • Search engine optimization (SEO)
  • Analytics and data-driven decision making
  • Email marketing and campaign management

This course provides the tools, strategies, and insights needed to thrive in today's digital landscape. Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your skills, this course offers something for everyone.
